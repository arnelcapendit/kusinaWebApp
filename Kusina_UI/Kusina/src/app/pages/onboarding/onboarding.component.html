Who are we?

InfraMetrics supports CIO’s web analytics services. We let you track near real time key performance indicators such as visits, downloads, keywords, and many more. We also let you track your application's performance in CIO NOW (App 358 – 3 mostly used page, 5 commonly used page and 8 rarely used page) once your application is onboarded to us.

How to avail our services?

We can have a quick discussion with your team on your current analytics/reporting needs that our tool may be able to provide or other use cases that you may require in the future.

Grafana

Grafana is open platform for data analytics and monitoring purpose dashboard and graph composer which runs as a web application. It is a frontend for creating queries and storing dashboards using data from Graphite and other data sources.

Inframetrics - new UI

This is our custom tool with brand new user interface that aims to give you near-real-time data for your web metrics. Soon, This tool also aims to have a self service functionality.

Performance Monitoring

CIO Performance Monitoring also known as the “App 3/5/8” model, in which the response time are measured using both the server and the end user. Pages visited by 80% of all users should load in under 3 seconds (mostly used page), Pages used commonly should load in under 5 seconds, and Pages used rarely, by support staff, or reports should load in under 8 seconds
